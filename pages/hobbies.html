Roller skating

When I was 8 years old I started skating classes. Since the moment I put the rollers on for the first time, it became one of my biggest passions. For many years, I did Artistic roller skating, and even took part in several competitions. However, 3 years ago I decided to make a change and buy inline roller blade shoes, in order to start skating in the streets of the city (Inline skating) and use them as an additional workout, and sometimes even as a way of transportation.

Painting

Even though I am a huge fan of Math and science, I've always had an artistic side that really characterizes me. I have loved painting, drawing and design in general since I was a little girl. A few years ago, during covid, I found an old denim jacket in my closet and started painting it in order to combat boredom. A few months later, some friends came to my house and saw that same jacket. They went totally crazy and started asking for one for themselves. That is how I discovered that my two passions, fashion and art, could be combined into not only a hobbie, but also an entrepeneurship. That is how I started my own brand of hand-painted one of a kind denim jackets back in Uruguay, which gave me the possibility making a living out of a hobbie.
